Panacakes

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Melted Butter
  • Egg
  1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk wet ingredients in separate bowl then add dry ingredients to wet ones.
  3. Stir until mixture is homogenous.
  4. Heat butter in a skillet and once hot, drop two or three tablespoons of batter for each pancake, making sure pancakes do not spread out.
  5. Leave batter on hot skillet until fluffy. Flip and cook on the other side for a minute.
  6. Enjoy the pancake.
